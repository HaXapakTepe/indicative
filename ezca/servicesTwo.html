INSTAGRAM ADS

It’s a formidable mistake to ignore Instagram advertising. 

While Instagram is smaller and has less audience compared to Facebook, its fame has grown significantly since coming online in 2010.

  • With more than 1.2 billion monthly active users and over 500 million active users daily, Instagram now stands among the most popular social networks globally.
  • Over 60% of users are more interested in a brand or product after seeing it on Instagram, and 1/3 have used the app to make a purchase.
  • More than 25 million brands have already created Instagram accounts, and 80% of users are following at least one. One-third of the most-viewed Instagram Stories come from businesses, with one-in-five receiving a direct message from its viewers.

There’s simply no better way than Instagram to tell the story of your business.

It doesn’t matter if you’re new to the app or are unhappy with your current approach, our Instagram advertising pros know how to create and execute compelling campaigns calculated to showcase your brand, inspire your audience, achieve your goals, convert clicks into sales, and guarantee the best possible return on your investment.

WHY INSTAGRAM
MARKETINGIS SO EFFICIENT

What’s behind Instagram’s effectiveness? It’s really all about the visuals.

Because Instagram is part of Facebook, Instagram advertisers have access to the Facebook Ads Manager. This multipurpose platform lets any business create and test highly targeted ads, track their most important metrics, and optimize content to yield the best possible results.

The Instagram environment also boosts organic growth for companies of all sizes, excluding the need to pay for likes and followers. By performing a regular posting schedule to raise brand awareness, you’ll build more natural relationships with prospective consumers and make your audience more receptive to your ads.

Instagram is also an exceptional tool for tracking the continually changing likes and dislikes of your audience. Because you can quickly estimate what’s drawing their attention, your business can better customize both organic and advertising content to encourage more engagement with your target customers.

And finally, your business can make sense of the information you collect from Instagram across all of your marketing efforts. Any website traffic coming from the app is easily retargeted to platforms like Google and Facebook, and you can even use Instagram to promote your other social media campaigns.

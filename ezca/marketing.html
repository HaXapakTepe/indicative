The ground-breaking virality of TikTok videos has altered the digital marketing scene. On top of that, TikTok is more popular than Twitter and Snapchat, and it’s also less filled with ads than its bigger rivals.

TikTok Advertising lets any brand — large or small — free its creative side to grow. And while the channel is a must for businesses targeted towards GenZ, more and more GenXers, Millenials and Boomers log on to the app daily. 

TIKTOK ADVERTISING:

FREE YOUR BRAND’S CREATIVE SIDE

The moment you’ve combined TikTok’s huge reach with the app’s adaptable ad formats, it becomes insanely easy to engage millions of potential customers around the world through creative and genuine brand content

Brand Takeover Ads

appear full-screen as soon as a user opens TikTok. They’re engaging right away, can’t be missed, and include a link to direct potential customers. This format is a great option for any business looking to raise brand awareness on a large scale.

#01

TikTok TopView Ads

appear as the first video three seconds after a user opens the app. TopView ads occupy the full screen, feature up to 60-seconds of video with auto-play and sound, and include a custom link.

#02

In-Feed Ads

allow businesses to integrate brand content in a user’s "For You Page." By including several call-to-actions, you’ll motivate prospective customers to shop, download your app, or visit your website straight from TikTok.

#03

Branded Hashtag Challenges

allows TikTok users the ability to participate and create content around your campaign theme. To get their Challenge Hashtag trending, advertisers can either team up with famous TikTok creators to encourage their followers to get on board or pay to have the challenge appear on the app’s Discover banner.

#04

Branded Effects Ads

and Snapchat Lenses are alike. They let marketers create custom sharable stickers, filters, and special effects. TikTok Branded Effects can go viral naturally, or advertisers can encourage users to create content using a custom filter in exchange for a shoutout or prize.

#05

Similar to Facebook and Instagram, TikTok is also a powerful platform for Influencer Marketing. But while Instagram videos use heavy editing, makeup,professional studio lighting and camera work, TikTok users share experiences in the raw, making for more relatable and authentic Influencer content.
